Welcome to Primitive Painter

Primitive Painter is playful teaching program for kids.  It helps them to use the mouse properly and guides them into the world of colours.

Installation and uninstallation
top
Run setup.exe. The wizard will guide you to complete the installation.
To uninstall, run "Start"-"Program"-"Primitive Painter"-"Uninstall". The uninstallation will be proceed automatically.

 
Play with your children
top
run "Start"-"Program"-"Primitive Painter"-"Primitive Painter". Click "previouse" or "next" button using mouse to select your favorite picture; click to choose one of the fifteen colours on the palette; then click on the blanks in the picture to fill then with the colour you have chosen.

Click "minimize" button to minimize the window and "close" button to close the window.
Remember, you can play two of the pictures freely with a unregistered version as you will be asked to register with other pictures. To play all the pictures and get the ability of update your picture library, you should click the "Register now!" button to register your software. For details, see Registration section.

Update library
top
There are fifteen pictures distributed with the main program. The full version of Primitive Paint has the ability to update the picture library without any charge. You are supposed to access our website every few weeks to find if there are new pictures available. Once a picture package available, you may download it to your local hard disk. Click the "update" button to open the importation window as follows:

Click "browse" button to browse your hard disk to locate the picture package you have downloaded. Then click the "Import" button and reopen the game window to complete the procedure.
